Who am I?

I am imaginative and a dreamer.

I live in a small town on the island Funen in Denmark. I am twenty years old and currently I am studying multimedia design.

My love for design, especially graphic design, has been with me since I was little. I went to a kindergarten that followed a certain viewpoint called Waldorf education or Steiner education. I know this may sound like some crazy brainwashing sect for children but don't worry it really isn't. The Waldorf education is a pedaogy that focus on developing the imaginantion and artistic abilities in kids. I remember all the toys in the kindergarten was made of natural materials such as wood and the dolls were without faces which led room for the imagination to make up what the dolls looked like. This is where my creative mindset, love for design and imaginative nature springs from.

Since I am quite the daydreamer and care for details, I love making designs that has a stong feel and message to them. It is important to me that the design I create is able to touch the viewer/target audience. Making the targeted audiece feel your message makes the design great in my eyes.
